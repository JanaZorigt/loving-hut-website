About Us

Loving Hut was created with a vision that all beings could live in peace, love and harmony with each other and the planet. It is an invitation to gourmet cuisine made with wholesome vegan ingredients, offering an accessible starting point for those making the noble transition to a plant-based diet. We have over 60 items on our menu including gluten-free options, raw options etc. We put lots of love and careful thought into all we do. We hope you enjoy all that we have to offer, and share the experience with others.
